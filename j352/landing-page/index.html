Whiskers is a state of the art cat caffe that serves delicous coffee and tea along with baked treats. We house shelter cats from all over the state that shelters have had trouble finding forever homes for. At Whiskers, you can enjoy the company of our amazing kitties while doing work, spending time with friends or family, or simply enjoying a cup of coffee. All of the cats that you will meet here are adoptable so you may end up coming for coffee and then going home with your new best friend! Whether you are looking to adopt, or just love cats, we would love to have you visit us here at Whiskers!
